What is UGEE?

The Undergraduate Engineering Entrance Examination (UGEE) is an entrance exam for admission to B.Tech programs at several prestigious universities. It is designed to evaluate the candidate's knowledge in core subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English.

Key Highlights

  • Conducted annually.
  • Tests students on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English.
  • Online examination with multiple-choice questions and problem-solving scenarios.
