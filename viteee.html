What is VITEE?

The VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEE) is conducted by VIT University for admission to its B.Tech programs. It is one of the most sought-after engineering entrance exams in India.

Key Highlights

  • Conducted annually.
  • Tests students on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English.
  • Online examination with multiple-choice questions.
