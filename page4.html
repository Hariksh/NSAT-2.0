Exam FAQs

Your comprehensive guide to exam preparation and rules

Technical Issues

Internet Disconnection

Notify your instructor or the exam coordinator immediately via email or the provided communication channel. Most platforms have an auto-save feature, so your progress may be preserved.

Exam Platform Not Loading

Try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Clear the browser cache and cookies.
  • Try a different browser.
  • Restart your device.
  • Contact technical support if problems persist.

Power Outage

Inform your instructor or invigilator as soon as possible. Some exams allow rescheduling or provide a window to reattempt based on valid reasons.

Exam Rules

Tab and Application Switching

Do not switch tabs or use other applications during the exam. Such actions may be flagged as suspicious activity.

Exam Breaks

Check exam rules carefully. In proctored exams, leaving your seat may be prohibited. Plan your time to avoid breaks if they are not allowed.

Allowed Tools

Tool usage depends on exam guidelines. Always refer to instructions provided before the exam starts.

Exam Content

Ambiguous Questions

Attempt the question based on your understanding and make a note to inform the instructor after the exam.

Accidental Submission

Contact your instructor immediately. Some platforms allow re-entry or submission correction within the exam window.

Partial Marks

Refer to exam instructions. MCQs may have no partial marks, but descriptive answers might be graded for partial credit.

Preparation and Setup

Pre-Exam Checklist

  • Stable internet connection
  • Fully charged device or power backup
  • Required software compatibility
  • Proper lighting and quiet environment

Asking Questions

For most exams, avoid content-related questions. For technical issues, reach out to support as instructed.

Results and Feedback

Result Announcement

Results will be announced according to exam guidelines. Check official communication channels like email or online portal.
