What is JEE Main?

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main is one of the most competitive engineering entrance exams in India, conducted by the NTA. It is the gateway to prestigious institutes like NITs, IIITs, and other top engineering colleges.

Key Highlights

  • Conducted twice a year.
  • Focuses on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • Essential for JEE Advanced eligibility.
